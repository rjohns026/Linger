For individuals

The sermon was good. You can already feel it fading.

You're trying to be present — really present — but there's a lot coming at you. The pastor is moving fast. Someone's coughing. There's a slide with four scriptures on it. You had a thought that felt important and then it was gone. By Monday, what's left is a feeling, not the thing itself.

For churches & organizations

You've probably noticed it — people holding their phones up to photograph a slide, or furiously typing while the pastor is mid-sentence. They're trying. But the tools they're using weren't built for this. And for the rest of the congregation, the words just wash over them and disappear.

Linger gives your whole congregation a way to engage more deeply — not just during the service, but all week. How it works

Three streams. One document. Nothing missed.

Linger listens so you don't have to. Your only job is to show up.

01

Arrive & connect

Open Linger and select your church. If your church is partnered, you're automatically connected to a studio-quality audio stream. If not, tap once to start local recording from your phone.

02

Worship, undistracted

The app runs quietly in the background. Tap a single button to bookmark a moment, flag a question, or jot a thought. Linger handles the rest — every word, every scripture, every slide.

03

Notes, delivered

Minutes after the service ends, a structured document arrives: the sermon's main points, scripture references with full text, your personal annotations in context, and discussion questions for the week ahead.
